Author: Kathleen Joan Reichs

Description: Dr. Temperance Brennan is Director of Forensic Anthropology for the province of Quebec. Brennan's job is to find out the identity of bodies too destroyed for any other expert to identify. And her latest case is a little messy. The body of a dismembered woman is found in an abandoned monastery. This isn't particularly unusual for Brennan but something makes her compare the body of this woman to other cases. There's some kind of pattern. But can she convince Detective Claudel before the bodies build up?
